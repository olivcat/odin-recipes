Ramen Noodle Soup

Description

This delicious ramen noodle soup is a wonderful winter warmer.

Best enjoyed steaming hot using the best quality ingredients you can find.

Add your favourite hot sauce for an extra kick and a sprinkle of black sesame seeds and a handful of chopped spring onions for that extra texture

Ingredients

  • 3 Â½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. Combine broth and noodles in a medium saucepan; cover and bring to a boil over high heat, stirring to break up noodles.
  2. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chilli oil, and ginger.
  3. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions.
